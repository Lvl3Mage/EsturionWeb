DELIVERY AND PAYMENT

How long does delivery take?

  • Spain – 1 working day (we ship from Monday to Friday inclusive) / possibility of delivery on Saturday.

  • Balearic Islands – 2 working days (we ship from Monday to Friday inclusive) / possibility of delivery on Saturday.

  • Portugal – 1-2 working days (we ship from Monday to Friday inclusive) / possibility of delivery on Saturday.

  • France – 2 working days (we ship from Monday to Wednesday inclusive).

  • Other European countries – 2 working days (we ship from Monday to Wednesday inclusive).

How much does delivery cost?

  • Spain and Portugal — Discount of 13 € on delivery for orders equal to or exceeding 200 €.

    For orders below this amount, the cost of delivery is automatically calculated depending on the number of boxes and weight.

  • Balearic Islands — Discount of 20 € on delivery for orders equal to or exceeding 300 €.

    For orders below this amount, the cost of delivery is automatically calculated depending on the number of boxes and weight.

  • France — Discount of 26 € on delivery for orders equal to or exceeding 250 €.

    For orders below this amount, the cost of delivery is automatically calculated depending on the number of boxes and weight.

    The minimum order amount to France is 90€.

  • Other European countries — Discount up to 50 € to delivery for orders equal to or exceeding 300 €.

    For orders below this amount, the cost of delivery is automatically calculated depending on the number of boxes and weight.

How can I pay for my order?

  • You can pay by credit card – we accept all cards except American Express, as well as payments through PayPal.

    In Spain and Portugal, it is possible to pay in cash to the courier.

  • In case of a return:

    – Refunds are processed within a maximum of 5 working days.

    – We will refund you in the same way you paid (except for cash on delivery, in which case you will need to provide a bank account number for the transfer).
